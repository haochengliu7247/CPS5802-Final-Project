Qwen2.5-7B and Llama 3.1 8B were selected as teacher models because they are strong open-weight instruction-tuned models that can be run locally, are substantially larger than the Qwen2.5-0.5B student, and represent two different model families. This enables comparison between Qwen-based, Llama-based, and multi-teacher distillation while keeping the workflow reproducible and feasible on available GPU resources.
